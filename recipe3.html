Bruschetta with Tomato and Basil
Ingredients

  • 1 French baguette, sliced
  • 4-5 ripe tomatoes, diced
  • 1 cup fresh basil leaves, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup extra-virgin olive oil
  • Balsamic glaze for drizzling
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Method

  1. Preheat Oven
    • Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Prepare Baguette Slices
    • Arrange the sliced baguette on a baking sheet. Toast the slices in the preheated oven until they are lightly golden and crispy.
  3. Prepare Tomato Basil Topping
    • In a mixing bowl, combine the diced tomatoes, chopped fresh basil, minced garlic, and extra-virgin olive oil.
    • Season the mixture with salt and pepper to taste. Gently toss until well combined.
  4. Assemble Bruschetta
    • Once the baguette slices are toasted, remove them from the oven.
    • Spoon the tomato and basil mixture onto each slice of baguette.
  5. Drizzle with Balsamic Glaze
    • Drizzle balsamic glaze over the bruschetta for an extra burst of flavor.
  6. Serve
    • Arrange the Bruschetta with Tomato and Basil on a serving platter.

Tips

  • For added richness, you can spread a thin layer of goat cheese or cream cheese on the baguette slices before adding the tomato and basil topping.
  • If you prefer, you can also brush the baguette slices with olive oil before toasting for a more pronounced flavor.

This Bruschetta with Tomato and Basil is a delightful appetizer, showcasing the freshness of tomatoes and the aromatic blend of basil and garlic. Enjoy this classic Italian dish as a starter for your next gathering!
